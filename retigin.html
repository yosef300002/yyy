Sayings of scholars and righteous people about repentance :

Ibn al-Qayyim said: “The heart becomes sick just as the body becomes sick, and its cure is in repentance and abstinence. It rusts just as a mirror rusts, and its polish is in remembrance. It becomes naked just as the body becomes naked, and its adornment is piety. It becomes hungry and thirsty just as the body becomes hungry, and its food and drink are knowledge, love, trust, repentance, and service.
